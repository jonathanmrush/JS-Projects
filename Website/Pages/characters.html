Their history

950 S.C.E

The land, a constant tug of war. The sea, boiling over with archaic power. Back and forth the villages are passed around like a used whore. Prizes to be won by not only the domineering countries but the faction held within their walls. Villagers pray to their gods hoping for this all to end, but deep in their hearts they know things can only get worse. This is the state of Tereniaâ€™s villages. You are someone who has decided it was in your best interest to take a trip to the only neutral settlement north of the Bad Lands. It has been a long journey that is almost impossible to do in large numbers, due to the hordes of beast and monsters roaming the hills. For whatever reason, the town of Mungdale has not had issues with these beasts and is known to be a resting place for weary travelers before heading to the Bad Lands. They are a very spiritualistic society who wish to not be part of any country. The Jay would like nothing more than to take their settlement and set up a stronghold against the Bad Lands, while the Schaus has maintained neutral relations.
